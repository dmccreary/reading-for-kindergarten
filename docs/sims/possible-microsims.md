Letter Recognition

  1. Alphabet Explorer - Interactive display of all 26 letters; click to hear the letter name and see uppercase/lowercase forms
  2. Letter Matching Game - Match uppercase letters to their lowercase partners
  3. Letter Hunt - Find and click specific letters hidden among other letters
  4. Letter Tracing - Trace letters with finger/mouse to learn letter shapes

  Phonemic Awareness

  5. First Sound Finder - Listen to a word, identify the first sound from choices
  6. Sound Counter - Count phonemes in spoken words by tapping/clicking
  7. Word Counter - Count spoken words in a sentence (one-to-one correspondence)
  8. Rhyme Time - Match words that rhyme together

  Letter-Sound Correspondence

  9. Consonant Sound Match - Click a consonant letter, hear its most common sound
  10. Vowel Sound Explorer - Interactive vowel chart showing short and long sounds
  11. Sound-to-Letter Match - Hear a sound, click the letter that makes it
  12. Letter Sound Keyboard - Virtual keyboard that plays sounds when letters are pressed

  Blending

  13. CVC Word Builder - Drag letters to build consonant-vowel-consonant words
  14. VC Word Blender - Blend vowel-consonant combinations (at, in, up)
  15. Sound Slider - Slide through sounds to blend them into words
  16. Word Machine - Input sounds, watch them combine into a word

  Sight Words

  17. Sight Word Flashcards - Interactive flashcards for 18 sight words
  18. Sight Word Memory - Match pairs of sight word cards
  19. Sight Word Bingo - Listen and find sight words on a bingo card

  Multi-Skill Games

  20. Letter Motion Maker - Associate arm gestures/motions with letter shapes (supports "Create"
  objective)
  21. Nonsense Word Generator - Build and sound out made-up CVC words
  22. Reading Progress Path - Visual journey through all skills with mini-challenges
